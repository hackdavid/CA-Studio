# Writing Style Rules

## Perspective

Your report must be written from a **third-person, observational perspective**.

## Banned Words & Phrases

**Do NOT use any of the following:**

| | | | |
|---|---|---|---|
| I | Me | My | Mine |
| We | Our | Us | Ours |
| Myself | Ourselves | We're | We've |
| I believe | In my opinion | According to me | I feel |
| As I see it | I'm | I think | Personally |
| I have | I will | We can | Our experience |
| From my point of view | In my perspective | As far as I'm concerned | I know |

## Approved Phrasing Examples

Use or model from the following third-person, observational phrasing:

- "The researcher" or "the observer"
- "It was observed that..."
- "The data suggest..."
- "The experiment revealed..."
- "The results show..."
- "One can infer that..."
- "The study indicates..."
- "The findings imply..."
- "The evidence supports..."
- "An analysis of the data"
- "The procedure was executed as follows..."
- "The measurements were taken..."
- "The subjects/participants behaved in a certain way..."
- "The test conditions were controlled..."
- "The trends in the data"
- "The observations were made by the team."
- "The process was documented as..."
- "The phenomenon was recorded."
- "A statistical analysis was conducted."
- "The data were collected and analysed."

# Front Matter Guidance

## Declaration

Certify that:

1. The report constitutes your own work.
2. Where the language of others is used, quotation marks so indicate, and appropriate credit is given.
3. The report describes original work that has not previously been presented for the award of any other degree at any other institution.

**Include:**
- Your name
- The date
- Your signature

## Acknowledgements

Thank the people who have supported this work and your studies in general. It is up to you who you thank.

## Abstract

**Word count:** Up to **500 words**

**Content:**
- A short summary of the entire project.
- Include: the **problem**, the main **literature reviewed**, your **implementation**, and your **findings**.

> **Tip:** Write the abstract **after** you have finished the entire report.

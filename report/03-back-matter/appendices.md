# Appendices

## Placement

Appendices appear **after references**.

## Purpose

Your appendices depend on the nature of your project. They are considered **additional information** and should not be relied upon to understand your main body of work.

Refer readers to an appendix using a phrase such as:

> "See Appendix A for further details."

## Required Appendices

The following documents **must** be included:

| Appendix | Content |
|---|---|
| **Appendix A: Project Proposal** | Your original project proposal. |
| **Appendix B: Project Management** | Evidence of your use of a project management tool. |
| **Appendix C: Artefact / Dataset** | A description and **link** to any technical output (dataset, code, etc.). It is **strongly recommended** you use GitHub or something similar. |
| **Appendix D: Screencast** | A **link** to the video recording demonstrating your artefact. Share via Microsoft Stream, OneDrive, or any method giving full access to your supervisor and second marker. |

## Formatting

- Use **Heading back matter style** for appendix headings.
- You can delete the template guidelines, but you need to leave the rest of the page blank with "Appendices" at the top.

## External Communications

For any important communications between you and external stakeholders:

- Remove private data.
- Anonymise communications.

## Note

A **PowerPoint is not required**.

# Chapter 5 — Conclusion

**Word count:** Up to **1500 words**

The conclusion summarises the project.

## What to Include

- Summarise the **overall outcome** of your project.
- State the extent to which the **aims and objectives** have been met.
- Highlight your **key outputs** and/or **discoveries**.

## Required Subsections

### Future Work

Answer: **What next?**

- You've completed a significant piece of work, but no project is ever 100% complete.
- You will have found new ideas along the way.
- If someone were to pick up your project, what avenues should be explored next?

> This section helps us understand what you have learned and shows you understand what a more ideal solution might look like, outside the constraints of the MSc Project timeframe.

### Reflection

You must **critically reflect** on the entire project process and how well you have worked on the project.

Consider:
- What particular things have you learned during the project?
- Why were you able and unable to meet project goals?
- What would you have done differently in hindsight?

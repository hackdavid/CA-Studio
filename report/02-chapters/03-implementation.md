# Chapter 3 — Implementation

**Word count:** Up to **3000 words**

Discuss how you applied the methodologies described above to your actual problem.

## Writing Strategies

- **Start with design work:** experimentations, system design/architecture, UX design artefacts, etc.
- **Use sprints as structure:** If you divided your work into sprints, that can be a good structure for this section.
- **Code snippets:** Only include code snippets for **particularly challenging** parts of your implementation.
- **Problem-solving narrative:** Pick out a few difficult problems you had to solve and tell us in detail **how you solved them**. This brings your experience to life.

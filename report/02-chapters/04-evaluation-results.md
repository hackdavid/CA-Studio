# Chapter 4 — Evaluation and Results

**Word count:** Up to **2000 words**

This is an important section where you **weigh up the strengths and weaknesses** of your artefact/experimentation.

## Evaluation Expectations

- If your project has a **user-facing element**, include some kind of **evaluation with representative intended users** (e.g., a "think aloud" usability test).
- Apply **standard metrics** for the domain you are working in and assess how you have performed against them.

> **Note:** Your project does not have to be perfect — indeed the outcomes might have been bad. The point is you **must evaluate the outcome** and discuss its strengths and weaknesses.

# Legal, Social, Ethical & Professional Considerations

## Expectation

All projects will have legal, social, ethical and professional considerations. You are marked on your ability to **identify them** and take any **actions necessary**.

## Guidance

- **Discuss with your supervisor** if you are not sure what considerations apply to your project.
- If your project required **ethical clearance**, this must be clearly stated in the relevant section, including **how the ethical considerations were managed**.
